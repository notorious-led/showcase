This retired cigarette vending machine will soon sell trinkets from local artists, makers, and craftspeople. Located on the second floor of Lowe Mill ARTS & Entertainment, this project is focused on creating a low-friction way for creators to share their work with the public, even if the creation was a one-off, a low-quantity run, or even something made for a laugh.

The vending machine currently accepts $1, $2, and $5 bills. (Its favorite is the $2 bill, obviously.) The machine does not give change, so count well. Please report any trouble with the machine to vending@notoriousled.com.

We are currently looking for people who are interested in selling stuff in the machine. If that's you, please review the info sheet for creators, or just email us (we're cool).
